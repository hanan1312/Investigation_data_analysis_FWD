Investigation Overview¶

(Ford GoBike) is a regional public bicycle sharing system in California's San Francisco Bay Area. It is operated by Motivate in a partnership with the Metropolitan Transportation Commission and the Bay Area Air Quality Management District. Bay Wheels is the first regional and large-scale bicycle sharing system deployed in California and on the West Coast of the United States. It was established as Bay Area Bike Share in August 2013. As of January 2018 & 2019, the Bay Wheels system had over 2,600 bicycles in 262 stations across San Francisco, East Bay and San Jose.

Dataset Overview¶

I took my data from this website "https://divvy-tripdata.s3.amazonaws.com/index.html" after cleaning The data contains 1698725 records and 16 columns, for the bike sharing system in year 2019, the most numerical data that matters are the (tripduration, and age) .. with categorical data in (gender, and usertype) and string data in the rest..I stored my new data after cleaning in a csv file called (new_data.csv) to work from it directly here.

A summary¶

As we can see a lot more subscribers using the bike sharing system than casual customers, both of them prefer ride the most during the summer season and the least during the winter months. Obviously different there are usage patterns and riding habits are observed between the two types of riders. Subscribers used the system heavily on work days, but according to gender males are using the system more than females, females mostly young although males older, both of genders prefere riding in work days than weekends, but after all old riders use the system in all seasons on the other hand younger ones prefer summer season more.
